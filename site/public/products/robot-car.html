FAQ Protocol

What age is this kit suitable for?expand_more
The Robot Car V4 kit is recommended for ages 10 and up. Younger builders (8-10) can use it with adult supervision. The step-by-step guide is designed with clear visuals for all skill levels.
Do I need any prior coding experience?expand_more
No prior experience needed! Our included course guides you from zero to autonomous navigation step by step. We use Arduino IDE and provide all code samples with detailed explanations.
How long does it take to build?expand_more
The complete build takes approximately 6-8 hours. We recommend spreading this over 3-4 sessions for the best learning experience. Assembly takes about 3 hours, and programming takes another 2-3 hours.
Are batteries included?expand_more
A battery holder is included in the kit. AA batteries are not included and need to be purchased separately. We recommend quality alkaline or rechargeable NiMH batteries for best performance.
Can I enter competitions with this kit?expand_more
Yes! The Robot Car V4 is certified for 5 competition categories: Pick & Place, Speed (Beam), NatCar, Sumo/Combat, and Soccer. It's the most versatile competition kit in our lineup.

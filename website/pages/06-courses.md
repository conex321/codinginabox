### Course 1: First Steps in Robotics

- **Difficulty:** Beginner (🟢)
- **Format:** Online + Offline
- **Ages:** 8-10
- **Duration:** 6 weeks (2 hours/week)
- **Kit Required:** Smart Water Dispenser ($26.65) or Solar Light Tracker ($21.24)
- **Description:** A gentle introduction to the world of robotics and electronics. Students learn basic circuitry, simple sensor concepts, and build their first working device. No prior experience needed.
- **What You'll Learn:**
  - What electricity is and how circuits work
  - Reading a simple wiring diagram
  - Connecting sensors and motors
  - Building a working STEM project from start to finish
- **Outcome:** Students complete the course with a fully built STEM kit and a foundational understanding of electronics.

---

### Course 2: Introduction to Coding with Arduino

- **Difficulty:** Beginner (🟢)
- **Format:** Online
- **Ages:** 10-12
- **Duration:** 6 weeks (2 hours/week)
- **Kit Required:** Any CodingInABox kit with a microcontroller
- **Description:** Learn the basics of programming using the Arduino platform. Students write their first lines of code, control LEDs, read sensors, and understand how software talks to hardware.
- **What You'll Learn:**
  - Setting up Arduino IDE
  - Variables, loops, and conditionals
  - Reading sensor data
  - Controlling motors and LEDs with code
- **Outcome:** Students can write basic Arduino programs and understand the connection between code and physical outputs.

### Course 3: Robot Car Programming

- **Difficulty:** Intermediate (🟡)
- **Format:** Online + Offline
- **Ages:** 10-14
- **Duration:** 8 weeks (2.5 hours/week)
- **Kit Required:** Robot Car Kit ($40.10)
- **Description:** Dive deep into robot car programming. Students learn line following, obstacle avoidance, speed control, and Bluetooth remote control. This course prepares students for robot car competitions.
- **What You'll Learn:**
  - Sensor calibration and threshold tuning
  - PID control basics for line following
  - Ultrasonic distance measurement
  - Bluetooth communication and remote control
- **Outcome:** Students have a fully programmed robot car capable of autonomous and remote-controlled operation. Competition-ready.

### Course 4: Drone Fundamentals

- **Difficulty:** Intermediate (🟡)

- **Ages:** 12-14
- **Duration:** 8 weeks (3 hours/week)
- **Kit Required:** Mini Drone Kit ($94.90)
- **Description:** Learn everything about drone technology — from construction to flight. Students build a drone, understand flight physics, and learn to fly safely. Covers both manual and basic autonomous flight.
- **What You'll Learn:**

  - Flight controller programming
  - Manual flight skills and safety
- **Outcome:** Students can build, program, and safely fly their own drone. Prepared for B1 (No-Code) competitions.
